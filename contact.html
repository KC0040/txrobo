Common Questions

How long does setup take?

Typically 1–3 business days. If you send venue photos ahead of time, we can pre-configure routes and cut on-site setup to just a few hours.

Is the 6-day trial really free?

Yes. No deposit, no credit card, no commitment. If it doesn't work for your business, we collect it. If it does, we'll discuss a service plan.

What if the robot breaks down?

We're Texas-based. Most issues are resolved remotely within hours. On-site support is available same-day or next-day across the DFW area.
