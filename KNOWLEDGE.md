## 各產業 FAQ

### 餐廳 / Food Service

**Q: 機器人會不會讓客人覺得奇怪？**
A: Actually the opposite — our restaurant clients report that customers love it. Kids especially get excited, and it becomes a talking point that brings people back. It's a novelty that works in your favor.

**Q: 如果機器人卡住怎麼辦？**
A: The LIDAR maps every obstacle in real time. If it encounters something unexpected, it safely stops and navigates around it. Our team monitors performance remotely and is on-site same day if anything needs attention.

**Q: 我們座位很多，機器人夠用嗎？**
A: One T9 or T10 typically handles 100-200 covers comfortably. For larger restaurants, two robots are common. During the free trial we'll assess your floor plan and recommend the right setup.

**Q: 員工會不會抗拒？**
A: The key framing is: the robot takes the boring running-back-and-forth. Staff can focus on table interaction, upselling, and hospitality — the work they actually enjoy. Retention often improves.

---

### 飯店 / Hotel

**Q: T10 的廣告螢幕可以顯示什麼？**
A: Anything you want — hotel promotions, restaurant specials, local attractions, event announcements, QR codes for loyalty programs. It's a moving billboard that works 24/7.

**Q: 多樓層飯店可以用嗎？**
A: Yes. The T9 and T10 both support multi-floor operation via elevator integration. Our team handles the elevator interface setup during deployment.

**Q: 客房送餐的流程怎麼運作？**
A: Guest calls front desk → staff loads the robot → robot navigates autonomously to the room floor → calls/messages the guest → guest retrieves items. No staff escort needed.

---

### 醫療 / Healthcare

**Q: S100 消毒效果有臨床認證嗎？**
A: The S100 uses hospital-grade UV-C wavelength proven to neutralize pathogens. Specific certification documentation can be provided — would you like me to have our healthcare specialist follow up?

**Q: 消毒時員工需要離開嗎？**
A: For UV mode, yes — the robot broadcasts a warning and waits for the area to clear before activating UV. Spray mode can operate with people present. The schedule can be set for overnight or low-traffic hours.
