Our Journey

THE TXROBO
TIMELINE

2019
EastWestNexus Founded

Started as a cross-border trade company connecting U.S. businesses with Asian manufacturing partners. Began evaluating the KEENON product line.

2021
First Robot Deployments

Pilot programs launched at Dallas-area restaurants during post-pandemic labor shortage. T9 proved immediately successful in hotel corridors.

Expanded coverage to Oklahoma, Louisiana, New Mexico, and Arkansas. Surpassed 200 active robot deployments. Introduced S100 disinfection units for healthcare clients.
